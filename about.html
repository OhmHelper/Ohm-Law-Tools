About This Site

Lab Calculators / Ohm-Law-Tools is a collection of free, simple online calculators for electronics and physics. The goal is to make quick calculations easy, fast, and accessible without ads, accounts, tracking, or complicated software.

Why this site exists

As someone who studies and works with electronics, physics, circuits, and engineering concepts, I found myself constantly needing quick tools for:

  • Ohm’s Law
  • Resistor combinations
  • RC/RL/LC calculations
  • LED resistor sizing
  • Physics formulas
  • Math for labs, schoolwork, and hobby projects

Instead of jumping between calculators scattered across the internet, this site brings all the most useful tools together in one clean, fast, lightweight page — no pop-ups, no trackers, no distractions.

Who runs this site?

The site is maintained under the username OhmHelper on GitHub. It’s intentionally kept simple and anonymous — the focus is on providing useful tools, not personal branding.
