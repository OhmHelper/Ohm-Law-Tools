Last updated: 2025-11-20

1. What this site does

This site provides free electronics and physics calculators. You can use the tools directly in your browser. You do not need to create an account or log in, and the site does not ask you to submit personal information.

2. Information collected automatically

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. Like most web services, the hosting provider may automatically collect basic technical information in server logs, such as:

  • IP address and approximate region

This information is generally used for security, abuse prevention, and aggregate statistics (for example, how many people visit the site). I do not personally attempt to identify individual users.

The calculators themselves do not intentionally set cookies or store personal data. Your browser may store basic preferences or cache files to make the site load faster. If you prefer, you can clear your browser cache and cookies at any time through your browser settings.

The site may contain links to external websites (for example, datasheets, documentation, or product pages). I am not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

6. Data security

This site is served over HTTPS, which helps protect data in transit between your browser and the server. However, no method of transmission over the internet is perfectly secure, so you should avoid entering sensitive personal information into any calculator or free-form field.
