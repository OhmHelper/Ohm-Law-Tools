Study Tools & Note-Card Ideas

Quick reference for what to put on small note cards for different classes.

Physics (Intro / Mechanics)

  • All 3–4 kinematics formulas
  • Work-energy theorem, power formulas
  • Momentum & impulse, conservation of momentum
  • Common free-body diagram patterns (incline, tension, friction)
  • Typical constants: g, k, G, etc.

Circuits / Electronics I

  • Ohm's Law, power relationships
  • Series / parallel rules for R, C, L
  • Voltage divider, current divider formulas
  • RC/RL time constants, RLC resonance
  • Thevenin / Norton idea summary

Calculus

  • Derivative rules (product, quotient, chain)
  • Integrals of basic functions
  • Common trig identities
  • Series forms (e, sin, cos) if needed
  • Key theorems: MVT, FTC

Tip: use both sides of the card and group formulas by topic so you can find them fast during exams.
